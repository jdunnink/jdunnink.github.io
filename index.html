About Codam

Codam Coding College is a programming school in the heart of Amsterdam, and part of the global 42Network of schools. The curriculum is project based and focuses on peer to peer learning. There are no books, teachers or classes; projects are evaluated and tested by other students. Initiating projects are written in C to foster a strong understanding of core programming concepts (memory management, algorithms, data structures, types). Advanced projects allow students to specialize within different fields; Students can choose projects about, for example, graphics rendering, advanced algorithms, system administration, web development or low level programming.
